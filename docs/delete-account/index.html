Account Deletion

Delete your Car Vault account

This page explains how Car Vault users can request deletion of their account and associated data, what is deleted, and what may need to be retained.

Version 1.0 Last updated: 2026-04-03 Contact: carvaultapp@gmail.com

English

How to delete your account inside the app

  1. Sign in to your Car Vault account.
  2. Open the Settings area.
  3. Open the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete Account Permanently.
  5. Re-authenticate if prompted, then confirm the deletion request.

This is the fastest method and is available directly in the app account settings.

How to request deletion if you cannot access the app

Send an email to carvaultapp@gmail.com with the subject line Car Vault account deletion request.

Please include the email address used for your Car Vault account.

For security, we may ask for a reasonable verification step before we complete a deletion request sent outside the app.

Data deleted after a confirmed request

When a deletion request is completed, Car Vault removes the following account-linked data that it controls:

  • Your Car Vault authentication account.
  • Your profile record.
  • Cars, services, documents, expenses, reminders, fuel entries, parking locations, checklists, trips, valuations, and achievements linked to your account.
  • Uploaded attachments and images stored for your account.
  • Local cached profile and collection data stored by the app on device, when deletion is initiated from the app.

Data that may be retained

Some information may need to be retained for a limited period where required for legitimate reasons such as fraud prevention, dispute handling, security, store compliance, tax, accounting, or legal obligations.

This may include records held by payment and platform providers such as Google Play, Apple, Firebase, or other infrastructure providers acting under their own policies and legal obligations.

Deleting your Car Vault account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play or App Store subscription. Subscription cancellation must be handled in the relevant store account.

Timing

We aim to process confirmed deletion requests within a reasonable time after identity verification and request review.

Romanian

Cum iti stergi contul din aplicatie

  1. Autentifica-te in contul tau Car Vault.
  2. Deschide zona Settings.
  3. Intra in sectiunea Account.
  4. Apasa Delete Account Permanently.
  5. Reautentifica-te daca ti se cere, apoi confirma cererea de stergere.

Cum soliciti stergerea daca nu mai poti intra in aplicatie

Trimite un email la carvaultapp@gmail.com cu subiectul Car Vault account deletion request.

Include adresa de email folosita pentru contul tau Car Vault.

Pentru securitate, este posibil sa cerem un pas rezonabil de verificare inainte de a finaliza o cerere trimisa in afara aplicatiei.

Date sterse dupa confirmarea cererii

Dupa finalizarea cererii, Car Vault sterge datele asociate contului pe care le controleaza direct:

  • Contul de autentificare Car Vault.
  • Profilul utilizatorului.
  • Masini, servicii, documente, cheltuieli, remindere, alimentari, locatii de parcare, checklist-uri, trip-uri, evaluari si achievements asociate contului.
  • Atasamentele si imaginile incarcate pentru cont.
  • Datele locale din cache pentru profil si colectii, atunci cand stergerea este initiata din aplicatie.

Date care pot fi pastrate

Unele informatii pot fi pastrate pentru o perioada limitata daca acest lucru este necesar pentru prevenirea fraudei, gestionarea disputelor, securitate, conformitate cu cerintele magazinelor de aplicatii, obligatii fiscale, contabile sau legale.

Aici pot intra si inregistrari tinute de furnizori de plati sau de infrastructura, precum Google Play, Apple, Firebase sau alti furnizori, conform propriilor politici si obligatii legale.

Stergerea contului Car Vault nu anuleaza automat un abonament activ din Google Play sau App Store. Anularea trebuie facuta din contul magazinului relevant.

Timp de solutionare

Incercam sa procesam cererile confirmate intr-un interval rezonabil dupa verificarea identitatii si analiza cererii.
