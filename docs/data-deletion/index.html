Data Deletion

Delete Car Vault data without deleting your account

This page explains how Car Vault users can request partial or broader deletion of their app data while keeping their account active.

Version 1.0 Last updated: 2026-04-03 Contact: carvaultapp@gmail.com

English

Delete data directly inside the app

Car Vault lets users delete many records directly from the app without deleting the account, depending on the section being used.

  • Cars
  • Services
  • Documents
  • Expenses
  • Fuel entries
  • Reminders
  • Parking locations
  • Checklists
  • Trips and valuations
  • Uploaded images and attachments linked to records

Request partial or broader data deletion

If you want us to delete specific app data for your account without closing the account, send a request to carvaultapp@gmail.com with the subject line Car Vault data deletion request.

Please include:

  1. The email address used for your Car Vault account.
  2. The type of data you want deleted.
  3. Whether you want partial deletion or deletion of all app content while keeping the account open.

For security, we may ask for a reasonable verification step before we complete a request sent outside the app.

Data that can be deleted without deleting the account

Depending on your request, we can delete user-provided app content linked to your account, such as vehicle records, maintenance records, documents, expenses, fuel entries, reminders, parking data, checklists, trips, valuations, achievements, and uploaded attachments.

Data that may be retained

If you keep your account active, some minimum account data may need to stay in place so the account can continue to exist and sign in correctly. This can include your authentication record, basic profile data, and necessary settings linked to the account.

Some purchase, subscription, fraud-prevention, security, dispute, tax, accounting, or legal compliance records may also need to be retained for a limited period where required.

Records held by platform or infrastructure providers such as Google Play, Apple, Firebase, or related service providers may be subject to their own retention rules and legal obligations.

Timing

We aim to process confirmed requests within a reasonable time after identity verification and review of the request scope.

Romanian

Stergerea datelor direct din aplicatie

Car Vault permite stergerea multor tipuri de date direct din aplicatie, fara stergerea contului, in functie de sectiunea folosita.

  • Masini
  • Servicii
  • Documente
  • Cheltuieli
  • Alimentari
  • Remindere
  • Locatii de parcare
  • Checklist-uri
  • Trip-uri si evaluari
  • Imagini si atasamente incarcate pentru inregistrari

Solicitarea stergerii partiale sau extinse a datelor

Daca vrei sa stergem anumite date din aplicatie fara sa inchidem contul, trimite o solicitare la carvaultapp@gmail.com cu subiectul Car Vault data deletion request.

Include urmatoarele informatii:

  1. Adresa de email folosita pentru contul Car Vault.
  2. Tipul de date pe care vrei sa il stergem.
  3. Daca vrei stergere partiala sau stergerea intregului continut din aplicatie, dar cu pastrarea contului.

Pentru securitate, este posibil sa cerem un pas rezonabil de verificare inainte de a finaliza cererea trimisa in afara aplicatiei.

Date care pot fi sterse fara stergerea contului

In functie de cerere, putem sterge continutul introdus de utilizator si asociat contului, cum ar fi masini, interventii, documente, cheltuieli, alimentari, remindere, date de parcare, checklist-uri, trip-uri, evaluari, achievements si atasamente incarcate.

Date care pot fi pastrate

Daca alegi sa pastrezi contul activ, unele date minime despre cont pot ramane necesare pentru existenta si autentificarea corecta a contului. Aici pot intra contul de autentificare, datele de profil de baza si anumite setari necesare contului.

De asemenea, anumite inregistrari legate de achizitii, abonamente, prevenirea fraudei, securitate, dispute, obligatii fiscale, contabile sau legale pot fi pastrate pentru o perioada limitata, daca este necesar.

Inregistrarile tinute de furnizori de platforma sau infrastructura, precum Google Play, Apple, Firebase sau servicii conexe, pot fi supuse propriilor reguli de pastrare si obligatii legale.

Timp de solutionare

Incercam sa procesam cererile confirmate intr-un interval rezonabil dupa verificarea identitatii si analiza exacta a cererii.
